The Ultimate New Puppy Checklist: Are You Ready?

Posted on September 5, 2025

Bringing a new puppy home is one of life's most joyful experiences. To ensure the transition is smooth for both you and your new furry family member, preparation is key. This checklist covers all the essentials you'll need to welcome them home.

Feeding Essentials

A growing puppy needs the right nutrition to thrive. Start with the basics to establish a healthy eating routine from day one.

  • High-Quality Puppy Food: Choose a formula specifically designed for your puppy's breed size.
  • Food and Water Bowls: Stainless steel or ceramic bowls are durable and easy to clean.
  • Training Treats: Small, tasty treats are essential for positive reinforcement during training.

A Safe & Comfy Home

Your puppy needs a designated space where they can feel safe and secure. This is crucial for house training and creating a sense of routine.

  • Crate: A crate provides a safe, den-like space and is an invaluable tool for house training.
  • Comfortable Bedding: A soft, washable bed for their crate and another for a common living area.
  • Baby Gates: To block off areas of the house that are not puppy-proof.

Walking & Training Gear

You'll be ready for those first walks and potty training sessions with the right gear on hand.

  • Collar or Harness: A lightweight collar with an ID tag is a must. A harness can be a great option to avoid pressure on their neck.
  • Leash: A standard 6-foot leash is perfect for training and daily walks.
  • Potty Training Pads: Useful for the initial stages of house training, especially in apartments.

Playtime & Enrichment

A bored puppy is a destructive puppy! Keep them engaged with a variety of fun and safe toys.

  • Chew Toys: Durable chew toys are essential for teething and redirecting chewing behavior.
  • Interactive/Puzzle Toys: These keep your puppy's mind sharp and prevent boredom.

Being prepared makes all the difference in creating a positive and loving environment for your new puppy. For any specific questions or guidance, our team at Friends' Kennel Hub is always here to help!
