About Our Kennel

Dedicated to the health, happiness, and well-being of every dog that comes through our doors.

Our Story

Friends' Kennel Hub was founded from a simple, yet powerful love for dogs. It all started with a small group of friends who shared a passion for animal welfare and saw a need for a caring, professional, and reliable pet service center in our community. We wanted to create a place that felt like a second home for pets and a trusted resource for their owners.

Today, we've grown into a full-service hub offering everything from expert breeding advice to safe boarding and positive reinforcement training. Our core mission remains the same: to ensure every dog is treated with the love, respect, and expertise they deserve.
